BOGOTA, 9 SEP 89 (AFP) -- [TEXT] THE PROSECUTOR IN CHARGE OF INVESTIGATING THE ASSASSINATION OF LIBERAL PRESIDENTIAL CANDIDATE LUIS CARLOS GALAN TODAY ISSUED AN ARREST WARRANT FOR JAIME VALENCIA MARTINEZ, WHO HAS BEEN CHARGED WITH MASTERMINDING THE CRIME.

THE JUDGE'S DECISION WAS BASED ON THE FACT THAT VALENCIA MARTINEZ OWNS THE OFFICE WHERE FIVE INDIVIDUALS CHARGED WITH CARRYING OUT THE ASSASSINATION WERE ARRESTED A FEW DAYS AGO.

IT WAS LEARNED THAT, AFTER THE FIVE INDIVIDUALS WERE ARRESTED, VALENCIA MARTINEZ TELEPHONED THE JUDICIAL POLICE TO OFFER $125,000 FOR THE RELEASE OF THE SUSPECTS, WHO WERE IDENTIFIED AS ALBERTO JUBIS HAZVUMB, ARMANDO BERNAL ACOSTA, NORBERTO MURILLO CHALARCA, PEDRO ZAMBRANO DELGADO, AND LUIS ALFREDO GONZALEZ CHACON.

VALENCIA MARTINEZ IS APPARENTLY LINKED TO FARMING ACTIVITIES AND THE EMERALD BUSINESS.

ACCORDING TO REPORTS, WHEN JUDICIAL POLICE AGENTS ARRIVED AT VALENCIA MARTINEZ' APARTMENT TO ARREST HIM, HE HAD ALREADY LEFT THE BUILDING IN A HURRY.
