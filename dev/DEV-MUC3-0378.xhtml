[CRISTIANI] I BELIEVE THE WORD NEGOTIATION HERE IN OUR COUNTRY NO LONGER HAS THAT GENERIC MEANING GIVEN BY THE DICTIONARY. THIS WORD HAS BEEN MANIPULATED BY LEFTIST FORCES IN THE SENSE THAT TO NEGOTIATE IS TO ACCEPT WHAT THE FMLN WANTS. ACCORDING TO THE LEFT, THAT IS WHAT NEGOTIATING MEANS. I BELIEVE THE WORD NEGOTIATION HAS A GENERIC MEANING. A NEGOTIATION IS TO TRY TO FIND JOINT AGREEMENTS TO SOLVE A PROBLEM AND THAT IS WHAT WE HAVE BEEN PROPOSING. WE HAVE SOUGHT AGREEMENTS ON THE LARGEST NUMBER OF POINTS POSSIBLE. LOGICALLY, NOT ALL POINTS WILL BE ACCEPTED BY THE OTHER SIDE AND WE BELIEVE THE FMLN IS MAKING A MISTAKE WHEN IT THINKS IT WILL NEGOTIATE ITS ECONOMIC, POLITICAL, OR SOCIAL PLATFORM WITH THE GOVERNMENT.
