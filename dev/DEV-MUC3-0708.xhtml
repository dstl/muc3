MEXICO CITY (MEXICO), 12 NOV 89 (EFE) -- [TEXT] THE SALVADORAN WORKERS NATIONAL UNION FEDERATION (FENASTRAS) TODAY ACCUSED ITS COUNTRY'S ARMY OF "UNLEASHING VIOLENCE AGAINST THE CIVILIAN POPULATION BY DROPPING ROCKETS AND BOMBS FROM SALVADORAN AIR FORCE AIRPLANES AND HELICOPTERS AND LEAVING A VERY LARGE NUMBER OF VICTIMS."

IN A COMMUNIQUE DELIVERED TO MEXICAN NEWS AGENCY "NOTIMEX," FENASTRAS CALLED ON "INTERNATIONAL ORGANIZATIONS AND HUMANITARIAN GROUPS TO DECLARE THE PLACES ATTACKED BY THE ARMY ZONES UNDER PROTECTION TO AVOID FURTHER BLOODSHED."

MEMBERS OF THE ARMY AND THE ALFREDO CRISTIANI GOVERNMENT HAVE CALLED FENASTRAS A FRONT ORGANIZATION OF THE FARABUNDO MARTI NATIONAL LIBERATION FRONT, (FMLN).

ON 31 OCTOBER, TERRORISTS ATTACKED THE FENASTRAS BUILDING IN SAN SALVADOR KILLING SEVERAL UNION LEADERS AND DESTROYING THE BUILDING. FENASTRAS MEMBERS IN MEXICO HAVE ACCUSED DEATH SQUADS RUN BY THE SALVADORAN ARMED FORCES THROUGH THE NATIONAL INFORMATION DEPARTMENT OF THE ATTACK. ON THE NIGHT OF 11 NOVEMBER, THE SALVADORAN GUERRILLAS LAUNCHED AN OFFENSIVE AGAINST MILITARY AND GOVERNMENT OBJECTIVES.
